Welcome to the Pacemaker User Guide

In this guide we're going to take you through the fundamentals of using Pacemaker. We've broken this guide into major sections which give you a good understanding of what Pacemaker is and how it works.

Introduction.

Pacemaker is conference management site, which contains information about past and upcoming conferences, their speakers and topics, locations and schedule of events and etc. Whole application is split into administrative and public parts. On the next chapters you will get detailed instruction how to use it.

Public Part

  1. Main conference menu

        On a header of main page of pacemaker you’ll see the menu with conferences which looks like a row of flags with years. When you click on year, will be displayed all conferences that have been this year and all flags with conferences of current year are always open.

  1. Call for speaker

        As a visitor, you can send a request to be a speaker on future conference which you choose.

        Step 1. You have to click on some ongoing conference on which you want to be a speaker. Then, scroll the page down and click the button “Call for speaker!”.
        Step 2. In a modal window enter your name and email and click send button.

Administrator of pacemaker will answer to your request as soon as possible.

        

  1.  Speaker Registration

You will be able to register at the web site, if you get invitation letter for registration at any conference.

Step 1. Check your mailbox. If you get invitation letter, click on the link inside to register.

Step 2. Fill in the following form with information about you. Enter all necessary data and click on Create speaker.

Step 3. You should enter information abour your speech(event) at the conference. Fill in the form with following information and click on Create event.

If you did all steps correctly, your registration is finished.

Remember, you can be register by this link only once.

 

 Administration Part

  1. Login Page and Home Page

This chapter describes how to access the administration console and the various elements that are part of the administration console user interface. You can access the administration console using a web browser.

Step 1. Open your browser and type the following URL: http://host:port/ login

During the setup process for the entitlement repository (or database), a default administrator account is created that can be used to access the administration console initially. The default administrator credentials are:

  • Login: admin
  • Password: 00000000

Step 2. Log in to the administration console using these credentials. Log in Page shows the Pacemaker login screen.

Step 3. After successful login, the home page is displayed, which contains list of all conferences that take or took place in Soft Serve, date and year of holding a conference, location, schedule of events and etc.

        

  1. Add New Admin Through Console

Adding new administrator without any pre registration on the website is very useful. You can run a simple rake task to create a new admin user through console

bash $ rake admin:create

Upon successful authorization go to step 1 of  paragraph Login Page and Home Page.

and login with credentials that you have just established.

  1. Create Conference

        To create a new conference, just click a button “New Conference” under the Conferences table. Than add an unique name of the conference, year of the conference, date, deadline date for speakers and quantity of attenders. In the date field appears a calendar with a possibility to choose time. Registration Deadline is a date when ends the possibility to became a speaker of the conference.

When you fill all fields and click “Create”, it will redirect you to the table with all conferences. At the top of the list is displayed next upcoming conference. Each conference has parameters of schedule, location, report, news, which you can see or create a new one. It is also possible to publish, edit or delete the conference.

  1. Invite Speaker

4.1.  How  to send invitation letter to speaker

You can invite new participants to the certain conference by sending them an invitation letter with a  signup URL.

Step 1. Choose Speakers on the dashboard, then press  Invite Speaker button on the bottom of the page.

Step 2. Enter speaker’s e-mail  in the field labelled Email, choose conference and  message (you can create a new or select one from  existing list of messages) to reply to (more info see at  3.2 .  Invitation Messages).

Step 3. Click on  Send.

Person who has received an invitation will be able to register on Pacemaker web-site by clicking on the invitation link in a browser. One person can be register by this link only once.

4.2 .  Invitation Messages

To choose an appropriate version of message which you would like to send, you need access to Invitation Messages tab on navigation bar. This page contains the list of all messages  which have been used by administrator for sending speaker’s invitation letter.

By default, messages are displayed in descending order of version. The last version of message is on the top of list and tagged as Activated. In case you want to send a message with context which was used in the past, you just need to find this message on the list and press on appropriate SwitchTo button.

            4.3.  Invitations

You can follow information about invitations.

Choose Invitations on the dashboard. You can see the next information: email of speaker (where invitation was sent to), status of invitation, time of sending and link to registered speaker (it is aviable in case if status is Registered or Complete).

The status is:

  •  new   when invitation was sent, but speaker hasn’t been registered yet.
  • Registered  when speaker is registered, but event hasn’t been registered yet.
  • Complete when speaker is registered and at least 1 his event is registered too.

You also can delete invitation, if it’s status is New. In this case, the registration link (in email, which user received) won’t be aviable and he will not be able to register at the site.

   3.     Manual Speaker Registration

You can register new participant by yourself.

Step 1. Choose Speakers on the dashboard, then choose New Speaker on the bottom of the page.

Step 2. Fill in the following form with information about speaker. Enter all necessary data and click on Create speaker.

                                   

4. Create news of conference

Step1. Visit the Conference area of Pacemaker administrator side and look for the “News” button of certain conference.

Step 2. After clicking, window appears with listing of news, where you can also find button “New News”.

Step 3. Then you can see a form, what you may fill out. You'll be asked to provide a name of your news, add a description and click “Create News” to finish. If you change your mind you can click “Cancel ” option and you'll redirect to News list page.

                 

         

5. Creating location

For creating location or report you need to be on conference listing page. To do this you need to click on button “Conferences” in main menu of Pacemaker site.

After that you need to click on a button “Create Location” in column Location.

After clicking on the button it takes you to a form for creating new location.

Field «Place Type» indicates the type of place for holding conference and field «Name» - its name. Fields «City» and «Address» indicates city and address of this place. When you enter the address in the field «Address» it will show the map with a marker that marks this place.

Using the drop-down list you can bind existing contacts to the current location.

It is also possible to delete a contact in case of a false choicing by clicking on button «Remove».

Field «How to find» indicates additional information, which may include data about place for holding conference, trams, trolleybuses and buses that can get to this place. The peculiarity of this field is that they connected with text editor wysiwyg, which allows you to format text with the following rules: change the font family, make text bold, italic and underlined, place text in the center and on the edges of the page, stretch the width of the page and add an images.

To save a location with filled data you need to press a button «Create Location», which is at the bottom of the form.

6. Creating report

For creating report you need to click on a button “Create Report” in column Report.

After clicking on the button it takes you to a form for creating new report:

Field «Responsible» indicates the person responsible for current report and field «Title» - name of this report. Field «Description» consist of information about the conference.

To save report with filled data you need to press a button «Create Report», which is at the bottom of the form.

For creating contact, you need to click on the button "Contacts" in main menu.

After clicking on the button you will be redirected to the contacts listing that displays all the information of contact persons: name, phone numbers, email addresses and skype. It is also possible to delete and edit them by clicking buttons on the right side of the listing.

7. Creating Contact

To create a new contact you need to press button «New Contact» in the bottom of the listing.

Then you will be redirected to the creation contact form.

Field «Name» and «Surname» indicates the firstname and lastname of the contact person. Field «Telephone» specified contact person's phone. It is also possible to specify multiple phones through the sign ";".

Field «Email» indicates the email address of the contact person. it also has ability to add multiple email addresses by pressing button "+"

In case of false adding extra fields there is an option to remove it. To do this, click on the button "-" near to the appropriate field.

Field «Skype» indicates the skype of contact person.

To save contact with filled data you need to press a button «Create Contact» which is at the bottom of the form.

8. Working with schedule

Administrator can use special page to manage the position order of events of the conference. There are “Schedule” buttons near conference name on conferences page.

On this page user can see all events of the conference. First number is time of the begining of event. Second number is the duration of event. After that we can see title of event and speaker.

User can use his mouse to drag elements and make the position order as he want.

Data sends to server after every change and position order saves on the server.

Green message means that all gone right.

Red message means that some errors was encountered and user must contact main administator.
